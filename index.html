About US

Welcome to The Sweet Escape, where every slice tells a story — and you get to be part of it. We’re not just a cake café. We’re a space to slow down, get creative, and celebrate the sweet moments in life. From handcrafted cakes and dreamy desserts to our signature “Decorate Your Own Cake” experiences, everything we do is about joy, creativity, and connection.

The Faces Behind the Frosting

Lena Yılmaz

A self-taught home baker from Izmir who began baking to reconnect with her grandmother’s old recipes. She believes that baking is a way of keeping family memories alive.

Tarık Demir

A retired firefighter from Ankara who found peace in baking after leaving a high-stress career. His layered cakes are precise and heartfelt, and he's always the first to offer help to newcomers.

Aylin Karaca

A pastry student living in Istanbul, juggling part-time work and culinary school. She’s passionate about baking and often shares snapshots from her compact kitchen setup.
